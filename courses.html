Teenage Driver Training

MEDALLION SCHOOL OF ADVANCED MOTORING pioneers the first structured Teenage Driver Program in Nigeria. Our goals for this innovative program is to teach young people the rules of road, the basic skills they need to control the car, and safe driving practices, such as defensive driving and risk assessment. Young people are therefore equipped with the tools and knowledge to be safer drivers, and give their parents/guardian peace of mind. In essence, we instill a safe driving culture into this next generation of drivers and prepare them for licensure upon attainment of age 18. This course is a structured attempt to ‘make –up’ the absence of a formal teen driving education curriculum in our High Schools in Nigeria (where we have majority of our teenagers). Special attention is taken to address teen’s tendency towards risk-taking and overconfidence, anger management skills, and avoid aggressive driving and to eliminate alcohol or other drug use while using a motor vehicle.

Convenient: Our teenage driver training programs are very convenient and are tailored to fit the schedules of our students. We avoid disruption to school, sports, family and religious activities. Missed classes can always be rescheduled. Private sessions can also be arranged. We also pick up and drop off our students at home, school and religious venues. Private in-car lessons are available to reduce stress and increase the productivity of the instruction. Our lecture rooms are also spacious, air-conditioned and have comfortable seating arrangement.

Medallion Diaspora Learners Program

With the integrity restored in the driver license processes under the DSSP, very many Nigerians living abroad earnestly desire to obtain the National Drivers License. Medallion provides such unique opportunity for them while conforming to the processes stipulated in the appropriate regulations.




Basic (Novice) Learners Program:

Driving is a serious business. In order to build responsible new and future drivers, MSAM takes the pains to put first hand learners (young and matured) through a thorough understanding of their duties and roles in keeping the road, themselves and other road users safe and secured. The course content is robust and contains the whole gamut of the FRSC approved curriculum for basic driver education.


Course under the curriculum include, but not limited to the following:

  • Basic Vehicle Mechanism
  • Knowledge of Road Signs and Markings
  • Knowledge of the Highway Code
  • Knowledge of Traffic Laws
  • Basic First Aid Practice, etc.

At the end of the program, successful students are issued a General Certificate of Driver Education, under the authority of FRSC, which qualifies the student to proceed to apply for and obtain a Nigerian Driver’s License Class ‘D’ Category. It is to our credit that majority of our students pass the requisite pre-license statutory theory and practical tests at their first attempts.

Medallion Accada Learners Program

Nigeria’s road crashes and fatality rate records are depressing. This is mainly due to a failure to impart sound and responsible driving culture into new and young drivers from generation to generation. The absence of Driver Education in our High School curriculum is a strong evidence of such institutional failure. Medallion attempts to fill this gap by providing students in tertiary institutions of learning the opportunity to receive sound and structured driver education at a special discount! Lessons are available at selected campuses all over the country, as well as any of our training centres close to students. This program prepares the new generation of drivers with the required skills, confidence, knowledge and discipline to navigate and relative effectively in the 21st century driving conditions, not only in Nigeria, but wherever in the world they may find themselves.

Features of the Medallion Accada Learners Program are

  • Flexible tuition payment plan
  • Generous discount on tuition
  • Pick-up and drop-off at gate of campus
  • Available to all students from age 18 to 3 years post-NYSC!
  • Further discount for group of 3 and above (trainings to be conducted as a group)
Imraa Mou’mina Learners Program

Medallion provides an exciting, secured and comfortable learning environment for females of all faiths who may be forbidden or may otherwise be uncomfortable being trained by a male instructor in a private tuition-vehicle or classroom, without the presence of the husband or a Mahram with the woman. This is specifically forbidden in Islam. Our Academy therefore offers lessons for women, provided by women. Our all-female faculty consists of experienced female instructors assisted by female staff complement, who help to boost the confidence and piety of their female folks in the course of learning.

Some of the features of the Imraa Mou’mina Learners Program include the following:

  • Dedicated all-female crew for classroom and in-vehicle training sessions
  • Pick-up and drop-off at home/office
  • Convenient classroom environment to take care of infants and toddlers
Driver Certificate of Professional Competence (CPC) Program

Medallion has designed this intensive training program to confirm, and expand on the existing knowledge and skills of occupational/professional drivers to ensure that they continue to be safe, courteous, eco-friendly and fuel efficient drivers. It will also enable drivers keep up-to-date with ever changing regulations in Nigeria and the various states in which they operate. This program aims to achieve the purpose of the Driver Certificate of Professional Competence (Driver CPC) introduced by the European Union but with extensive Nigerian content adaptation. It is also harmonized with the curriculum for the Advanced Professional Driver Training Course of Lagos State Drivers Institute (LASDRI) as well as the Executive Driver Course of the FRSC.
