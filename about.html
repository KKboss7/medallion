OUR MOTTO:

Safety. Confidence. Discipline.

We are a driver education and driver development establishment since 2012 when our parent company Medallion Intermediaries Limited took advantage of the innovative Driving School Standardization Program (DSSP) of the Federal Road Safety Commission (FRSC) as part of her efforts to improve the safety of Nigerian roads and entrench a responsible and informed driving culture in Nigeria.

are licensed to undertake driving school business all over Nigeria by FRSC, having been accredited after a thorough screening and pre-approval exercise. Apart from our FRSC certification, we are in collaboration with Lagos State Drivers’ Institute (LASDRI) established by the Lagos State Drivers’ Institute Law of Lagos State. Under the collaboration arrangement, drivers trained by our Academy in conjunction with LASDRI are issued with LASDRI certification/re-certification cards. The collaboration further grants us the privilege of utilizing the resources, facilities and equipment of LASDRI towards ensuring a safe driving culture in Lagos State and Nigeria in general, through the training and re-training of all categories of drivers. We are in similar relationships with road safety/driver training regulatory authorities in states all over Nigeria, including Oyo, Ogun, Osun, FCT- Abuja, amongst others.



OUR VISION

Our vision is to impart the most comprehensive education to all categories of drivers in Nigeria, be it Basic (Novice) Learners, Teenage Drivers, Professional/Commercial Drivers and High Capacity (Passengers/Goods) Drivers. We drive to achieve this by combining a formidable blend of resources, faculty and most modern array of equipment available in the world for the grooming, training and improvement of the skills and minds of all categories of drivers in Nigeria.
