Our Programs Upskill You With Driving Training

Teenage Driver Program

Our goals for this innovative program is to teach young people the rules of road, the basic skills they need to control the car, and safe driving practices, such as defensive driving and risk assessment.

  1. Beginner
  2. 3 Week
Basic (Novice) Learners Program

MSAM takes the pains to put first hand learners (young and matured) through a thorough understanding of their duties and roles in keeping the road, themselves and other road users safe and secured.

  1. Beginner
  2. 3 Week
Medallion Accada Learners Program

This program prepares the new generation of drivers with the required skills, confidence, knowledge and discipline to navigate and relative effectively in the 21st century driving conditions, not only in Nigeria, but wherever in the world they may find themselves.

  1. Beginner
  2. 3 Week
Medallion Diaspora Learners Program

With the integrity restored in the driver license processes under the DSSP, very many Nigerians living abroad earnestly desire to obtain the National Drivers License. Medallion provides such unique opportunity for them while conforming to the processes stipulated in the appropriate regulations.

  1. Beginner
  2. 3 Week
Imraa Mou’mina Learners Program

Medallion provides an exciting, secured and comfortable learning environment for females of all faiths who may be forbidden or may otherwise be uncomfortable being trained by a male instructor in a private tuition-vehicle or classroom, without the presence of the husband or a Mahram with the woman.

  1. Beginner
  2. 3 Week
Driver Certificate of Professional Competence (CPC) Program

Medallion has designed this intensive training program to confirm, and expand on the existing knowledge and skills of occupational/professional drivers to ensure that they continue to be safe, courteous, eco-friendly and fuel efficient drivers.

  1. Beginner
  2. 3 Week

Safety. Safety. Safety
Safety is our watchword at MSAM. Your teenage children/wards are in safe hands when they learn driving at any of our centres. Adult novice students, as well as experienced professional drivers enjoy the same degree of safety. Our learning environment, which includes the premises of the Lagos State Drivers’ Institute (LASDRI) provide a serene and regimented atmosphere for imbibing the rudiments of the art and science of driving.
Our Training Vehicles
We parade a fleet of training vehicles that complement our safety culture. All our vehicles are roadworthy, comfortable and well maintained. They are also properly fitted with appropriate safety devices to afford our students security and control and prevent accidents. In the unlikely event of minor injuries and illnesses during trainings, fully stocked first-aids kits are also included in the vehicles.
Our Faculty
Our faculty members have combined experience of over 100 years in the areas of driver training, road safety management, motoring law etc. They are certified driver instructors by FRSC and LASDRI. Our team also includes experienced public servants who have served in traffic management/road safety organizations, including LASDRI, FRSC and VIS,etc.
